What is Traceability?

Traceability is the ability to track the journey of a product through every stage of the supply chain, from origin to final delivery. It ensures transparency, quality control, and accountability across processes.

How Does Traceability Benefit Your Business?

Traceability strengthens brand trust, improves operational efficiency, ensures regulatory compliance, and enables quick response to quality issues. It opens access to new markets and builds a strong foundation for sustainable growth.

UtshoTracer
